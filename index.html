    Frequently asked
    How do I connect my Fitbit?
    Account → Connected devices → Add → Fitbit. Sign in to your Fitbit account and approve the read scopes. New data appears within ~2 minutes.
    Who can see my health data?
    Only you and providers you explicitly share with. The list lives in Account → Sharing & consent. You can revoke anyone instantly.
    Is GetVit HIPAA-compliant?
    Yes — GetVit operates under a Business Associate Agreement, encrypts data at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3), and is HITRUST CSF + SOC 2 Type II certified.
    How do I switch between Patient and Provider mode in this demo?
    Use the small Patient | Provider pill in the top-right of the header. Provider mode unlocks EHR / HIE and Population Models tabs.
    Can I export everything?
    Yes. Account → Export gives you a ZIP with PDFs, raw FHIR Bundles, and CSVs of all wearable streams. Delivered within 1 hour.
